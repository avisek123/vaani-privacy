# Privacy Policy for Vaani

Last updated: June 1, 2026

## Overview
Vaani is a private offline AI chat 
application. We are committed to 
protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection
Vaani does NOT collect any personal data.
All information stays on your device only.

## What stays on your device:
- Your display name
- Chat conversations
- Memory and facts you add
- App settings and preferences

## What we do NOT do:
- We do not collect your data
- We do not send data to servers
- We do not share data with third parties
- We do not use analytics
- We do not use advertising
- We do not require account creation

## AI Model
Vaani uses on-device AI models.
Your conversations are never sent
to any external server or AI service.

## Children's Privacy
Vaani does not knowingly collect
data from children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this policy occasionally.
Changes will be posted on this page.
