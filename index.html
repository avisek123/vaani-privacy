Vaani Privacy Policy

🔒 Your data never leaves your device

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Vaani is a private offline AI chat application. We are fully committed to protecting your privacy. All data stays on your device only.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Vaani does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. Everything stays on your device.

What Stays On Your Device

  • Your display name
  • Chat conversations
  • Memory and facts you add
  • App settings and preferences
  • Downloaded AI models

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not collect your personal data
  • We do not send data to any server
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking
  • We do not show advertisements
  • We do not require account creation
  • We do not access your contacts or files

AI Model

Vaani uses on-device AI models that run entirely on your phone. Your conversations are never sent to any external server or AI service. All processing happens locally.

Internet Usage

Vaani only uses internet connection for one purpose: downloading AI models when you first set up the app. After download, the app works completely offline.

Permissions

Vaani only requests permissions necessary for the app to function. We do not request access to contacts, camera, microphone, or location.

Children's Privacy

Vaani does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
